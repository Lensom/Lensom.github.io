music

Welcoming producers and performers from all over the world, our studios are practically
non-stop buzzing with music in all genres, from mainstream pop to indie bands.

At STMPD studios it’s all about the vibe. We strive to create the ultimate recording and mixing environment, a place where you feel at home right away.

The studio rooms are designed as a smart hybrid of modern digital production ease and vintage analog sound quality, equipped with the very best gear.

There’s a gorgeous instrument and backline collection, and of course our highly experienced engineers who work on world hits on a daily basis.
