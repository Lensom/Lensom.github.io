Exterior

There’s no second first impression. Exterior improvements provide the biggest return on investment. A new garage or swimming pool, energy-efficient windows, walkways, and fences are all popular. Three of the biggest trends are new, elegant entrances to add curb appeal; creative outdoor lighting to enhance landscaping; decks to extend living space. A prospective buyer first takes in the exterior look of the home and then registers the entranceway.

A welcoming entranceway – especially three-dimensional patios – creates a lasting impression, adding character. LED’s mimic fireflies, water lights resemble fish and solar and low-voltage systems add to outdoor lighting popularity. Decks provide extra living space, echoing interior elements, and increase re-sale value. Any yard can become a dreamland with creativity and vision. Your imagination is the only limit!
